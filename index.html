Why choose our eggs

  • High-quality protein: Eggs are an excellent source of complete protein, containing all nine essential amino acids needed for muscle growth and repair.
  • Food allergy-friendly: Eggs are not considered a common allergen, making them a good option for those with food allergies or sensitivities.
  • Brain health: The choline in eggs is important for brain development and memory, particularly during pregnancy and early childhood.
  • Heart-healthy fats: The yolk contains healthful monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats, which can help lower LDL cholesterol levels.
  • Affordable: Eggs are generally an affordable source of protein and nutrients, making them a cost-effective food choice.

Sustainability at Khabo's Happy Hen Farm : Committed to continuous improvement and environmental stewardship, we champion sustainable agriculture and community collaboration. Together, we're building a sustainable future. Join us in celebrating the earth's beauty with every egg you enjoy.
