Services Offered

At FreshEggs Farm, we're dedicated to providing a variety of services that cater to your needs and support our commitment to sustainable farming practices. Explore our offerings below:

Farm Tours and Educational Workshops

Immerse yourself in the world of sustainable agriculture with our guided farm tours and educational workshops. Learn about hen care, egg production, and environmental stewardship while enjoying a hands-on experience on the farm.

Community Engagement Events

Join us for community events that celebrate healthy living and sustainable agriculture. From farm-to-table dinners to cooking classes and volunteer opportunities, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Customized Egg Products

Need something unique? Our team can create custom egg products to meet your specific needs. Whether it's specialty eggs for baking or promotional items for your business, we'll work with you to bring your vision to life.

Sustainability at Khabo's Happy Hen Farm : Committed to continuous improvement and environmental stewardship, we champion sustainable agriculture and community collaboration. Together, we're building a sustainable future. Join us in celebrating the earth's beauty with every egg you enjoy.
